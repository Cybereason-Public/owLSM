After 8 months of hard work, we are excited to officially release owLSM.
owLSM is a first of its kind, eBPF LSM agent that implements a stateful Sigma Rules Engine.
We at @Cybereason, focused on closing gaps in the minimal and stateless prevention capabilities that projects like Tetragon & KubeArmor offer. 
If your company offers Linux/Cloud protection, we urge you try owLSM.
